## 💡 PINTEREST TIPS
- Use VERTICAL images (2:3 ratio works best)
- Add text overlay on images (use Canva — it's free)
- Post consistently (2-3 pins/day minimum)
- Use ALL 500 characters in description
- Include 3-5 hashtags
- Join group boards in your niche for more reach
- Pinterest is a SEARCH ENGINE — think SEO, not social media
