1. Log into Pinterest with businessforclawde@gmail.com
2. Go to: https://www.pinterest.com/business/convert/
3. Click "Convert to Business account"
4. This gives you analytics + rich pins (more clicks!)

## Step 5: Enable Rich Pins
1. Go to: https://developers.pinterest.com/tools/url-debugger/
2. Enter any blog post URL
3. Click Validate — this enables rich pins site-wide
