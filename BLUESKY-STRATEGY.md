# Bluesky Strategy for @prettypicked.bsky.social

## What Works on Bluesky
- Conversational, authentic tone — talk like a friend, not a brand
- No hashtags (Bluesky doesn't use them)
- Questions and opinions drive engagement
- Mix value posts with link posts (don't just spam links)
- Short, punchy posts perform best
- Community engagement matters — reply to people
- Proper grammar and capitalization (clean, not lazy)

## Post Types to Rotate
1. **Product recommendation** — single product, personal opinion, why it's worth it
2. **Blog link** — tease the content, link to prettypicked.netlify.app
3. **Engagement question** — "What's your favorite...?" / "Am I the only one who..."
4. **Hot take** — opinion on a trending product or beauty trend
5. **List post** — "3 things under $20 that changed my routine"
6. **Relatable moment** — "That feeling when you find the perfect product..."

## Posting Schedule
- 2-3 posts per day
- Morning (9-10 AM PST) — engagement question or relatable post
- Afternoon (1-2 PM PST) — product recommendation
- Evening (6-7 PM PST) — blog link or list post

## Tone Guide
- First person: "we" or "I"
- Casual but polished
- Enthusiastic but not fake
- Honest — mention cons too sometimes
- Emoji: use sparingly, 1-2 per post max
- Never say "affiliate link" or "ad" — just share naturally

## What NOT to Do
- Don't spam links every post
- Don't use hashtags
- Don't be corporate or salesy
- Don't post the same thing twice
- Don't ignore replies
